Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect the security of your personal data. Our security measures include:

Your data is stored in secure servers with restricted access controls.

Only authorized personnel have access to your personal information.

Your Data Protection Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have certain rights regarding your personal data:

Your Rights
